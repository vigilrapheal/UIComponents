Frequently Asked Questions

What is Crawly?

Crawly is a tool that crawls your website to find out broken pages to help you to maintain your project. Crawly has a powerful alerting system that if any page of your project is broken, you will get a notification. Please check our documentation for more information.

What are Internal and External links?

Crawly control your both internal and external links. An internal link is a link where located on your website and points to your website. Similarly, an external link is a link where located on your website but point to another website. You can fully control your web application with Crawly in confidence.

Can I Work with My Team?

We gladly support teamwork. If you are working on your project as a team, Crawly has super easy to use Team Management interface that you and your teammates can use. You can share your sites with team members without bother.

Can I have upper subscription?

Currently, we have two different subscription plans named Basic and Standard. The basic package is free for everyone with limited features. The standard plan is a paid plan that supports upper features. Soon we will come up with more features and advance business plans.
