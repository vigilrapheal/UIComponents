Frequently Asked Questions

What is SiteCx?

SiteCX is a tool that crawls your website to find out broken pages to help you to maintain your project. SiteCX has a powerful alerting system that if any page of your project is broken, you will get a notification. Please check our documentation for more information.

What are Internal and External links?

SiteCX controls your both internal and external links. An internal link is a link where located on your website and points to your website. Similarly, an external link is a link where located on your website but point to another website. You can fully control your web application with SiteCX in confidence.

Can I Work with My Team?

We gladly support teamwork. If you are working on your project as a team, SiteCX has super easy to use Team Management interface that you and your teammates can use. You can share your sites with team members without bother.
